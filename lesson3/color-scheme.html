Website Typography

For the Chamber Website, I chose the Roboto Condensed and Barlow Condensed fonts. They are similar looking to the font that appears in the Treasure Valley Chamber of Commerce logo on the home page, and I think they will fit the site well.

For them, that compulsion is far stronger than any fear. The fact that we find that exceptional is a sad indictment of the human race. I'd like to live in a world of heroes. If we did, there would be no wars. - General Jolyon Iver, Commander, COG Land Forces (Gears of War: Aspho Fields pg. 211)
