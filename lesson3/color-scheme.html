Website Typography

For the Chamber Website, I chose the Roboto Condensed and Barlow Condensed fonts. They are similar looking to the font that appears in the Treasure Valley Chamber of Commerce logo on the home page, and I think they will fit the site well.

Color Scheme

A total of nine, carefully selected, dimmed pastel colors for a eye-comfortable, but yet colorful ambiance.

The palettes providing different color effects for dark & light ambiance designs. It has been choice for following a minimal and flat style pattern.

It aims to ensure an undistributed focus on importants part of element, a good readability and quick visual distinction between the different elements.
