Target Audience

  • Who: Business Owners, residents and future residents interested in starting a business in Treasure Valley.
  • Income Range: $45,000 - $100,000+
  • Why: We believe that having access to resources and networking helps skyrocket businesses to the next level. The Treasure Valley Chamber of Commerce wants to help provide that to anyone looking to get started or bring their business operations here.

Personas

Rusty Shackleford

  • Age: 44
  • Business: Owner of Shackleford Tools
  • Why Treasure Valley?: The Treasure Valley has been a hotbed of new housing and business construction projects for the last two years with no signs of slowing down. Bringing his hardware store here will allow his business to further expand, and help the needs of the growing community.

Amanda Hugginkiss

  • Age: 25
  • Business: YouTube Creator
  • Why Treasure Valley?: The Treasure Valley has a booming fashion and beauty scene, waiting to be explored by the up and coming YouTube fashionista. Bringing her YouTube channel here will help show what Treasure Valley is like to thousands of people.
