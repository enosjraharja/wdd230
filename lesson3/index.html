Site Name

The full site name will be Alistair Chamber of Commerce. The short version will be Alistair Commerce.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Alistair Chamber of Commerce will be to improve the working relationships of local business owners and professionals in the city of Treasure Valley. It is also to help build and grow the local economy while improving the quality of life of residents of our great city.

Domain Name

The domain name will be AlistairCommerce.com.
